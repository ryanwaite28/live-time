Categories/Location

Categories are a way for users to find artists, venues and events! To properly enter categories, just make a comma-separated list! For example:

hip-hip, rap, reggae, soul, etc...

It cannot be more than 250 characters.

Same thing with location, you can make it a comma-separated list. For example:

Los Angeles, CA, California, etc...

Others can also find you by location!

Account Types

Artist
If you are looking to get booked and find places to perform, choose ARTIST.

Venue
If you are looking for artists to perform at your venue, choose VENUE.

USER
If you are looking for venues to attend and want to keep up to date with events, choose USER.

You can change your account type at any time. However, doing so may affect how this app works for you. For example, changing from ARTIST/VENUE to USER means people cannot search for your account. See the info page for more details.
